Privacy Policy – YT Transcript Pro

Last updated: 23 December 2025

YT Transcript Pro does not collect, store, or transmit personal user data.

1. Data Collection

The extension does not collect personally identifiable information, health data, financial data, authentication data, location data, browsing history, or user activity.

2. How the Extension Works

YT Transcript Pro operates locally within the user's browser. Upon explicit user request, it reads transcripts already available on YouTube and allows exporting them in selected formats.

3. Permissions

Browser permissions are used exclusively to provide the extension’s core functionality, such as accessing the active YouTube tab, exporting files, and storing user preferences locally.

4. Data Sharing

The extension does not sell, share, or transfer user data to third parties.

5. Cookies and Tracking

The extension does not use cookies, tracking technologies, or remote analytics.

6. Changes

This privacy policy may be updated if the extension functionality changes.
